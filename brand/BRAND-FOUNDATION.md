# itz_litny massage therapy — Brand Foundation (shared spec)

> The single source of truth. Website, portfolio book, and all collateral build from this.

## Positioning
A newly-credentialed massage therapist (Brittany) with an artist's sensibility. The brand fuses
**an artist's eye with healing hands** — color, intuition, and self-love translated into bodywork.
Natural, organic, holistic. Calm, nurturing, meditative. A genuine safe space.

## Name & wordmark
- Brand handle / wordmark: **itz_litny** (lowercase, soft)
- Descriptor lockup: *itz_litny — massage therapy*
- Practitioner: Brittany

## Tagline
- Primary: **"Come home to your body."**
- Supporting: *where an artist's eye meets healing hands*
- Alt: *Awoken, yet at ease.* (nods to her ♒ Aquarius bio)

## Voice & tone
Warm, grounded, unhurried, affirming. Second person, present tense. Embodied, sensory language;
never clinical or salesy. Examples:
- "You're safe here. Let's slow down together."
- "Sixty minutes that belong entirely to you."
- "Healing doesn't have to be earned — it's already yours."
