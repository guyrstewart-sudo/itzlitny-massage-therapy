01  /  Welcome

An artist’s eye,
healing hands.

You’re safe here. Let’s slow down together.

itz_litny began the way most good things do — quietly, and from the inside out. Brittany is a newly-credentialed massage therapist who came to bodywork already fluent in another language: color, intuition, and the slow craft of making something with her hands.

That artist’s sensibility is the whole point. She reads a body the way a painter reads a canvas — noticing where the light gathers, where the tension lives, what wants to soften next. The result is bodywork that feels composed rather than clinical: unhurried, attentive, and entirely yours.

This book holds the brand that grew from that practice — the words, the colors, and the feeling we return to every time. A genuine safe space, translated into a system.

“Healing doesn’t have to be earned — it’s already yours.”
The feeling
Natural · organic · holistic. Calm, nurturing, meditative.
The promise

03  /  Who She Serves

For the ones
ready to soften.

itz_litny is for people who suspect that rest is allowed — and want a practitioner who treats it that way. Different bodies, one shared wish: to feel at home again.

The over-carried
Caregivers, parents, and the quietly depleted who give all day and rarely receive. Here, you’re the one being held.
The reawakening
Anyone reconnecting with their body after stress, burnout, or a long stretch of just getting through. We start gently.
The expecting
Prenatal clients who want supported, knowledgeable, deeply comfortable care through a changing body.
The seekers
Wellness-minded folks drawn to natural, holistic, intuitive care — and to a space that feels like art, not a clinic.
“Whoever you are when you walk in, you’re welcome to set it all down.”
