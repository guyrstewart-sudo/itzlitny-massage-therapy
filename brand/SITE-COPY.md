## 3. ABOUT — Meet Brittany

**Eyebrow**
The hands behind the work

**H2** *(2 lines)*
A working artist,
a credentialed healer

**Body**

For years Brittany worked in color, line, and light — learning to notice what most people move past. That same attention now lives in her hands. She came to massage the way an artist comes to any new medium: with patience, curiosity, and reverence for the form.

Each session is composed rather than performed. She reads the body the way she reads a canvas — feeling for tension, for held breath, for the places asking to be tended to. Her touch is intuitive, unhurried, and deeply present.

Energy and alignment are the quiet philosophy beneath everything she does. The room is dim and warm, the breath slows, and the work becomes a kind of sacred ritual — an hour that belongs entirely to you.

**Value pairs**

- **Holistic** — She tends to the whole of you, not a single sore muscle.
- **Intuitive** — Her hands follow the body's lead, not a fixed script.
- **Grounded** — Calm, steady, and fully present, from first breath to last.
